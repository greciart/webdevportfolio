## Cotiza Guate: High-Performance Tech Branding & WordPress Development 🇬🇹
### The Vision: Merging National Identity with Modern Tech
For Cotiza Guate, the challenge was to build a reliable, fast, and visually striking platform for technology price comparisons in Guatemala. The client required a dual Dark & Light Mode interface to enhance user comfort during extended browsing sessions.

![Branding showcase of the Cotiza Guate logo, inspired by the national bird and currency of Guatemala.](/images/projects/cotiza-guate-quetzal-logo-identity.webp)

I spearheaded the full corporate identity, including the logo design. The branding is inspired by the Quetzal, Guatemala's national bird and currency, symbolizing trust and local financial authority. The UI reflects this through a sleek, tech-oriented aesthetic, using the logo's gradients to define the visual hierarchy.

## Technical Execution: Scalable WordPress & Elementor Architecture 🛠️
To ensure a balance between high-end design and client-side manageability, I utilized WordPress and Elementor. This combination allowed for:

**Dynamic Theme Toggle:** A seamless Dark/Light mode implementation that adapts to user system settings or manual preference.

**Custom Catalog Logic:** A structured system for 5 core pages: Home, Catalog, Search Results, Product Detail, and Contact.

**Optimized UX Flow:** A frictionless journey from the centralized search bar on the homepage to the final technical quote request.

![A comprehensive UX wireflow and information architecture diagram for CotizaGuate, mapping the seamless user journey from initial technology search to the final quotation request.](/images/projects/cotiza-guate-user-flow-architecture-logic.webp)

## Strategic UX Flow: Engineered for Conversion 🗺️
The navigation was designed to minimize "Search Fatigue":

**Instant Discovery:** A prominent search module for immediate product location.

**Adaptive Filtering:** A "Silo-structured" results page that allows users to drill down by technical specifications.

**Lead Capture:** A direct, high-converting contact funnel that turns product inquiries into actionable business leads.

The heart of Cotiza Guate lies in its visual identity. By integrating the Quetzal—Guatemala's symbol of freedom and its national currency—into the logo, I bridged the gap between a modern tech platform and national reliability. This deliberate branding choice resonates with local users, transforming a standard search engine into a familiar, high-authority tool. The fluid lines of the bird mirror the speed of the platform, while the colors transition from deep tech-blue to vibrant greens, signaling both innovation and local roots.

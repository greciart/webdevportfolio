## Technical Specifications & Tools 📋
**Core Stack:** WordPress + Elementor Pro (Custom Containers for Performance).

**E-commerce:** WooCommerce with specialized store-comparison logic.

**CRM & Automation:** Mailchimp integration for automated lead nurturing.

**Booking System:** Embedded Calendly for seamless schedule management.

**Speed Optimization:** Advanced Lossless Compression for images and zero-video background architecture.

**Security:** Private User Account Dashboards with role-based content visibility.

## The Client’s Vision: Elegance Meets Engineering Authority
The objective was to build a sophisticated platform where electronics engineering students could transition from browsing free projects to purchasing specialized courses and components. The client demanded a clean, professional aesthetic with a strict requirement: maximum loading speed without the use of video.

To achieve this, I focused on a motion-inspired UI driven by sliders and carousels, allowing users to navigate through high volumes of content and products without visual fatigue. Every asset was subjected to extreme compression while maintaining visual integrity, ensuring a lightning-fast LCP (Largest Contentful Paint).

## Strategic User Experience: 4-Level Permission Architecture 🔐
A cornerstone of this project was the implementation of a tiered content access system. To foster community growth and loyalty, the site features a manual membership hierarchy:

**Subscriber:** The default entry point for new users.

**Enthusiast:** For active community members.

**Student:** Access to specific educational modules.

**Professional:** Exclusive entry to advanced technical data and projects.

I configured a custom Login & Registration ecosystem where users can manage their personal profiles, while the administrator manually promotes users based on their engagement levels, creating an exclusive "club" feel that drives retention.

## Core Platform Features & Integrations 🛠️
**Dual-Purpose Storefront:** Powered by WooCommerce, the shop allows for the simultaneous sale of digital courses and physical electronic hardware, featuring a unique Store Comparison module.

**Educational Funnel:** Integrated Calendly modules allow students to book free consultations directly within the UI, significantly reducing the friction between the brand and the learner.

**Content Authority:** A robust Blog & Project Portfolio filtered by specialized categories, allowing the client to showcase technical expertise and improve Organic Search (SEO) visibility.

**Global Connectivity:** Direct WhatsApp integration for instant support and a Mailchimp Newsletter sync to nurture the leads generated by the site's high-intent search module.

## Project Architecture: Sitemap & UX Flow 🗺️
### Scalable Sitemap Architecture
I designed a Silo Structure that separates the "Learning" (Courses/Blog) from the "Buying" (Store). This logical hierarchy ensures that search engine crawlers can easily categorize Cedir as an authority in both electronics education and retail.

### The "Content-to-Conversion" UX Flow
The user journey is engineered for education-led sales. A typical path involves a user finding a free project via the Main Menu Search, booking a consultation via the Calendly Integration, and eventually purchasing a full curriculum through the WooCommerce Checkout.
